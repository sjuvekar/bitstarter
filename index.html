What is Coin of Life?

Coin of Life is a game based on Conway's 'Game of Life'. You start by placing some coins on the board and hit 'Play'. The coins accumulate according to these rules:

  • If a cell has 2 or 3 neighboring coins, it generates a new coin
  • otherwise, the coin in the cell disappears
